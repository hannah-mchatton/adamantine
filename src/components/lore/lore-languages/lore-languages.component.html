Some languages are closely related enough that someone who can understand one of them can understand bits and pieces of another, even if they can't actually understand the second language. These languages are called "sister languages". If you hear speech in the sister langauge of a language that you know, you can make an Intelligence (Society) check to attempt to understand the speech.

Common

Because Common is an amalgamation of other languages, speakers of Common can pick up on a few words. However, checks made in this regard are made at disadvantage.

  • Dwarvish
  • Elvish
  • Giff
  • Halfling
  • Jiganti
  • Sylvan
  • Vedalken
Faerie Languages

These languages, which are all to some degree descended from Sylvan, contain flowing words and lots of silent letters.

  • Elvish
  • Goblish
  • Kenderspeak
  • Squeak Speak
  • Sylvan
  • Undercommon
Primeval Languages

These languages are intensely spoken, with a great deal of inflection and tonality.

  • Draconic
  • Primordial
Psionic Languages

These languages involve a great deal of psionic prowess, and as such are so different from other languages as to be nearly a distinct category.

  • Deep Speech
  • Gith
  • Quori
Shadowy Languages

These languages, spoken by the denizens of the Lower Planes, are all extremely difficult to learn and master, as they are so different from most other languages.

  • Abyssal
  • Carcerin
  • Infernal
Steppe Languages

These languages consist of rough, hard sounds and relatively longer words than most other languages.

  • Dwarvish
  • Giff
  • Gnomish
  • Halfling
  • Jiganti
