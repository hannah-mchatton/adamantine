Astral Plane

The Astral Plane is less a plane and more a structural mesh that binds the universe together. While most see the Astral Plane as somewhere visited by those projecting their spirits, the true Astral is a multitude of countless manifestations all existing in tandem. Each of these manifestations is itself the Astral Plane in its entirety, but each has differing rules and properties depending on which manifestation it is. A non-exhaustive list of astral manifestations includes:

The Deep Astral

Sometimes considered the true Astral Plane, the Deep Astral is the underlying power behind the Astral Plane. One could think of it as the multiverse's source code. It requires immensely powerful magic to reach the Deep Astral, so much so that only powerful deities usually possess enough strength. However, any creature within the Deep Astral that learned to harness it could wield its fog to rewrite the multiverse as they see fit.

The Surface Astral

The Surface Astral, also called the Astral Fog, is what most mortals mean when they refer to the Astral Plane. It can be accessed through the Astral Projection spell or similar means, and manifests as a shallow reflection of the Deep Astral, an endless plane of roiling fog. Color pools can occasionally be found here, which will transport those who enter them to various other planes.

The Surface Astral has little in the way of true natives. Astral Dreadnoughts can be found here, as can a few other creatures, but it is usually a cold, lonely plane.

The Astral Sea

The Astral Sea, also called the Rainbow Ocean or Spellwaves, is the manifestation visited by spelljammers. Existing as the only truly three-dimensional manifestion, it is a massive ocean of a strange magical substance called phlogiston. This substance, not quite a liquid and not quite a gas, is what spelljammers ride upon in the Astral Sea. It is extremely volatile, so spelljammers have to be careful to not set it alight lest their entire ship burst into flames.

The Astral Sea is densely populated by both crystal spheres and native species such as scavvers, aartuk, and githyanki. More details can be found in the Astral Sea tab.

The Ethereal Plane

The plane of ghosts, the Ethereal Plane exists in tandem with the material, manifesting much like the Surface Astral. It is easily accessed by spells such as Etherealness, and contains all manner of spirits as well as other native lifeforms like phase spiders.

Extradimensional Spaces

Though they may not seem it, all extradimensional spaces such as those created by bags of holding, portable holes, or the Mordenkainen's Magnificent Mansion spell, are in fact individual manifestations of the Astral Plane. This is why attempting to join two bags of holding can have disasterous effects. These types of manifestations were never designed to be compatible with one another, and bringing them together causes ripples to echo through the Deep Astral.

Dimensional FTL Systems

Many spheres with advanced technology possess some form of faster-than-light travel. Those that use a dimensional system, such as Forcespace's hyperspace or Hammerspace's warp, are actually tapping into the Astral Plane to do so. When they travel to their alternate dimension, they are in fact traveling to a unique astral manifestation bound to their crystal sphere.
