At 1st level, and again at 4th, 8th, 16th, and 20th levels, you gain a number of Genius Points equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 0). If your Intelligence modifier increases, you retroactively gain Genius Points for past levels.

At 1st level, and again whenever you gain a level, you can expend any number of Genius Points to gain any of the following features of your choice.
