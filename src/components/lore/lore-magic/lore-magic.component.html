Fonts of Magic

These are some of the known fonts, or sources, that magic can draw upon. Typically, spells and other effects from the different sources of magic do not treat the others as being magical at all, or interact with one another.

Weavemagic

Weavemagic is the most commonly used by the residents of the Astral Realm. It draws upon the Spellweave, a mystical construct woven by Mystryl. Some mistakenly believe that divine magic used by deities does not draw upon the Spellweave, but most in fact do.

Voidmagic

Voidmagic, also called Astralmagic or Truemagic, draws directly on the energy of the Astral Plane. Very few creatures can wield Voidmagic, and it breaks most of the known rules of magic in everything it does. Many things that are impossible by design within Weavemagic are possible or even easy within Voidmagic. It is thought that the reason the Netherese were a particularly dangerous threat to the sanctity of magic and to Mystryl herself is that they managed to tap into Voidmagic in part.

Uvuumagic

Uvuumagic, also called Othermagic or Farmagic, is the magic used by the invaders from the realm of Uvuu. Uvuumagic is significantly more powerful in Uvuu, but still dangeous within the Astral Realm. It is Uvuumagic that enables the strange, dream-like qualities of the beholders or the space-warping abilities of the Uvuudaum.
